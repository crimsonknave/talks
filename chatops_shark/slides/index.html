Who Are We?

Developers who like automation, chat and devops.

What Is ChatOps?

  • Automating repetitive tasks
  • Making tools centralized and visible

Why ChatOps?

  • ChatOps encourages collaboration
  • ChatOps helps onboarding
  • ChatOps assists in triage
  • ChatOps automation removes human errors
